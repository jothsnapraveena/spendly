Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic profile information such as your name, email address, and password. As you use the service, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide Spendly's core functionality: logging your expenses, generating category breakdowns, and showing spending summaries over time. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible through your authenticated account. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active, and you may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery services) to operate the application. These providers process data only to the extent necessary to deliver their service and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to the Spendly team through the contact details provided on our website.
